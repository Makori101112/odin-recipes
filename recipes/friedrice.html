Fried Rice:

Description

Savor our delightful fried rice; a quick and satisfying Asian classic. Stir-fried with veggies, eggs, and a tasty sauce, it's a versatile dish that's perfect on its own or with added protein. Enjoy this flavorful and convenient meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold and day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil (such as vegetable or canola oil)
  • 1 cup diced vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, bell peppers, and corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sliced green onions for garnish

Steps:

  1. If you don't have cold, day-old rice, cook the rice according to package instructions and then spread it out on a baking sheet to cool for about 30 minutes. Cold rice works best for fried rice.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the diced onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and sauté for an additional 30 seconds. Stir in the diced vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Push the cooked vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the empty side. Scramble the eggs until they're fully cooked.
  5. Now, add the cold cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Mix it with the scrambled eggs and sauté for a few minutes to heat through.
  6. Drizzle the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using) over the rice and stir to evenly distribute the sauce. Continue cooking for a few more minutes, allowing the rice to absorb the flavors.
  7. Season the fried rice with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle sliced green onions on top for garnish.
  8. Your homemade fried rice is ready to be served as a delicious and satisfying meal. You can also add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein.
